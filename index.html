NEWSTAR FOOTBALL ACADEMY

Be Happy. Be Healthy. Be Alive.

The Purpose

  • To teach the pupils the game from the fundamentals.
  • To identify the talents.
  • To promote good health through sports
  • Create opportunity for recreation
  • To promote the inter-personal relationship amongst football players.
  • Adding values to the life of pupils.
  • To promote grass-root sports development

Target


The target for this football academy is to teach professional football from age of 5 to 15 year boys and girls.

The Development Program

  • Short Term Plan (First 6 Months)
    1. Teach the young boys and girls the game of football from the foundation. Teaching them the basic skill in the game of football which include : passing, controlling the ball , heading, dribbling etc.
    2. Give the players proper drills, which would make them physically and mentally fit.
    3. prepare the puplis for friendly (exhibitions) match with other schools in Thrissur.
  • Long term plan (second 6 months and beyond)
    1. Intense training
    2. More practice (exhibitions) matches
    3. Enroll the team in district league team
